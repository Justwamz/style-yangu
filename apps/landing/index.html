Clear design briefs — no more WhatsApp confusion
Receive a complete brief with the consumer's fabric photo, chosen silhouette, avatar render, occasion, and measurements. Every order starts with full context.
Deposit protection via M-Pesa escrow
Deposits are held securely by the platform and released when the consumer confirms collection. No more chasing payments or losing deposits.
Portfolio rendered on professional AI avatars
Your completed work displayed on diverse African model avatars — no photographer needed. First impressions that match your actual skill.
Cobblers matched to size-specific consumers
If you make custom shoes to any size, you are matched directly to consumers whose size is underserved by standard retail — extremely high purchase intent.
Escrow protection
Your deposit is safe before you cut a single thread
Consumers pay a deposit through Style Yangu before you begin work. Funds are held securely and released when the order is collected and confirmed. No more lost deposits, no more unpaid work.
Appointment booking
Measurement sessions at your workshop
Consumers book in-person measurement appointments at your listed location directly through the app. Measurements saved to their profile for every future order.
Order tracking
From brief to collection — every step tracked
Mark orders through Received, In Progress, Ready for Collection. Automatic WhatsApp notifications keep your clients informed at every stage.
Ad boost
Reach matched consumers directly
Boost your portfolio pieces or specialisations to free-tier consumers whose style, size, and occasion match your craft. Starting from KES 100 per week.
