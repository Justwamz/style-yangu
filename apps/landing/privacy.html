Data We Never Collect

  • Facial identity data — faces are never captured for identity processing or stored in any identifiable form.
  • Biometric data beyond skin tone analysis (stored as a colour profile, not biometric data).
  • Full phone contact lists.
  • Social media account content beyond what you explicitly upload within the app.
  • Financial account details beyond what M-Pesa STK Push authorisation requires.

How We Use Your Data

  • To personalise outfit suggestions, shopping recommendations, and messages from Amara or Kofi.
  • To match sponsored content (Ad Boost) based on style profile, body type, skin tone, size, and wishlist activity.
  • To operate the platform including storefronts, order management, and escrow services.
  • To send transactional communications via email and WhatsApp Business.
  • To calculate and pay referral and affiliate commissions.
  • To improve our AI models using anonymised and aggregated data only.

We do not sell personal data to third parties. We do not share personal data with external advertisers — all ad targeting is internal and based on Style Yangu profile data only.

Ad Targeting and Consumer Rights

  • Sponsored content shown to free-tier consumers is matched using your Style Yangu profile data. This data never leaves Style Yangu systems.
  • You may opt out of personalised ad targeting — sponsored cards will then be shown by general category only, not individual profile matching.
  • You can remove ads entirely by upgrading to the Premium tier.
  • Ad impression data is used only for frequency-cap enforcement and seller analytics. Sellers see aggregate counts, never individual consumer identities.

Your Rights Under the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019

  • Right to access — request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Right to correction — request correction of inaccurate personal data.
  • Right to deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated personal data.
  • Right to data portability — request your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Right to object — object to processing for specific purposes, including ad targeting.

To exercise any of these rights, contact privacy@styleyangu.co.ke. Style Yangu is committed to compliance with the Kenya Data Protection Act 2019 and the Computer Misuse and Cybercrimes Act 2018.
